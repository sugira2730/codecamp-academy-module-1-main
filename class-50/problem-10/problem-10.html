Problem 10

Challenge Exercise: Parking Fee System

Description

A parking garage wants to determine how much a customer must pay depending on the type of vehicle and the number of hours parked.

Inputs

  • Vehicle type (car or motorcycle)
  • Number of hours parked

Rules

Input Validation

  • If the hours parked are less than or equal to 0 → "Invalid parking time"
  • If the vehicle type is not "car" or "motorcycle" → "Invalid vehicle type"

Parking Fees

Cars

  • 2 hours or less → $5
  • More than 2 hours → $10

Motorcycles

  • 2 hours or less → $3
  • More than 2 hours → $6

Example

Vehicle type: car

Hours parked: 3

Output

Parking fee: $10
